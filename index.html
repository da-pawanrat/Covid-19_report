Covid 19 Situation Report, Thailand

Bangkok

Bangkok is the capital and most populous city of Thailand and has a population of over eight million, 12.6 percent of the country's population.

Phuket

Phuket is one of the southern provinces of Thailand. It consists of the island of Phuket, the country's largest island, and another 32 smaller islands off its coast. It lies off the west coast of Thailand in the Andaman Sea

Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai is the largest city in northern Thailand and the capital of Chiang Mai Province. It is 700 km (435 mi) north of Bangkok near the highest mountains in the country.
